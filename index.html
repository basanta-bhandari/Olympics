Why I Love the 90s Web

Posted on: January 8, 2026 at 11:22 AM PST

The 90s internet had CHARACTER. Every website was unique, weird, and unapologetically expressive. No algorithms. No minimalism. Just pure creativity and chaos.

People would spend weeks learning HTML just to make their favorite band's fan page. Webrings connected communities. The "Under Construction" GIF used to be sign of dedication, not laziness.
